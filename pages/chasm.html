Introduction

Demo of my first attempt at creating a game with Unity

The little cute car is literally terrible to drive, and gameplay is also not fun at all. However, it's still a memorable experience.

Additionally, the game is primarily made up of various plugins and art assets.
EVP5: Vehicle construction
GeNa: Scene layout
Pegasus: Camera track construction
FreeFlyCamera: Flying camera
EasyRoads3D, 3D Haven: Road construction

Thanks to this plugin, I can step in the game development world with a very low threshold.
