Legend of Light

Research on the design of video games for the treatment of Amblyopia



Introduction

This is an attempt at a serious game.

I summarized relevant research on electronic game therapy for amblyopia and summarized the interactive modes of treatment. Finally, I combined these interactive modes with role-playing games to enhance the fun of treatment.

Legend of Light is based on the I-BiT theory, using red-blue glasses as a foundation, adjusting game materials to present visual stimuli separately for amblyopic eyes. The test system is based on Long To's research, introducing a measurement mode for the degree of amblyopia, and based on theories such as fine visual training, incorporating special design elements into the game screen. Finally, the game uses a rogue-like mode to provide an independent gaming experience for each treatment session while limiting the duration of each game.

The game materials are sourced from the Unity store and AI tools. The project is not for commercial use.
